ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management

The ABM strand is designed for students who are interested in business, finance, and entrepreneurship. It focuses on developing skills in leadership, decision-making, problem-solving, and financial management.

Students study subjects such as accounting, business mathematics, marketing, and economics. These subjects help them understand how businesses operate, how money flows, and how to make smart financial decisions.

They also participate in activities like creating business plans, conducting market research, and presenting ideas. These experiences help them develop confidence, teamwork, and the ability to think strategically.

The ABM strand prepares students for college courses related to business administration, accountancy, marketing, and management. It is ideal for students who want to start their own business or work in corporate environments.

Career Paths

Students who take ABM can pursue careers such as accountant, entrepreneur, business manager, marketing specialist, financial analyst, or sales manager.

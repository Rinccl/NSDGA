Greenheights Campus

Greenheights Campus can be found at #98 Soliven St, Nangka, Marikina, 1800 Metro Manila. It is the largest campus of Nuestra Señora De Guia Academy of Marikina that is home to multiple strands; Grade 11 HUMSS, Grade 11 ICT, Grade 11 to Grade 12 EIM, and Grade 11 to Grade 12 BPP-FBS. The Greenheights Campus is the main campus for Senior High School students that provides everything a Senior High School students like Registrar work. The campus is also the main practice grounds for Crimson Guardian Atheletes.

The campus features a huge parking lot that stretches from the back and besides the seating for the campus' canteen. A spacious library with variety of books can also be found at the second floor of the building right beside the Faculty room. A large and spacious gym can be found at the 3rd floor or the highest floor of the building, which is used for P.E. classes and Crimson Guardian Athelete Trainings. The campus also provide specific room for specific strand that are necessary to achieve complete learnings such as Computer lab for ICT Students, Electrical room for EIM Students, and Cooking room for BPP-FBS Students.

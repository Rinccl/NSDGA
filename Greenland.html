Greenland Campus

The Greenland Campus is the only campus that hosts K-12 Students, it is the home of Kinder to Grade 12 students. The Greendland campus specifically accomodate the ABM strand rom Grade 11 to Grade 12. Greendland Campus is the first campus ever built by Nuestra Señora De Guia Academy and the oldest among the four. It can be located at 7 Washington St, Nangka, Marikina, 1800 Metro Manila

The campus features two huge buildings, the main building is where most of the Junior High School students go to and the newly built second building that mainly serves the Senior High School Students. Inside the main building, you are met with a big lobby, spacious canteen, and many rooms that goes 3 storey high. In the second building, a large computer lab can be found at the ground floor and a TLE lab is built on the scond floor of the building. The second building also hosts Grade 11 and Grade 12 ABM students. The two buildings connected by a wide open parking lot that can be also turned into a basketball court.

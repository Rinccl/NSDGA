J.P. Rizal Campus

The school campus is designed exclusively for STEM students in Grades 11 and 12, and its layout reflects a clean, modern, and organized atmosphere. Classrooms are arranged in long corridors, each room equipped with sturdy tables, whiteboards, and a smart television that can be used for visual aids.

Inside the campus is the canteen, a bright and lively space where students gather. Inside the canteen is a small café that stands out for its warm lighting and cozy seating. The café offers a comfortable corner where students can relax, enjoy snacks, or simply take a break. Its modern design, paired with the aroma of fresh coffee and pastries, gives the campus a more inviting and friendly feel. The campus can be found at 904 J. P. Rizal St, Nangka, Marikina, 1808 Metro Manila. It can be found near the main road.

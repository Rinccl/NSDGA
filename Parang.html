Parang Campus

Parang Campus is the home for both Grade 12 ICT and Grace 12 HUMSS Students. It is the only campus that specifically hosts Grade 12 Students. It is located at M455+89C, C.M. Recto St, Marikina, 1800 Metro Manila. The campus resides inside a peaceful and quiet street that improves the learning environment by a lot.

Out in front of the campus is a large parking lot that can fit a variety of vehicles. When you go inside the gate, a roomy lobby will greet you that also leads to a spacious canteen where students can enjoy a hearty meal. The rooms are connected by a long corridor, these rooms are occupied by Grade 12 ICT and HUMSS Students. At the 3rd floor, a huge events room can be found where all of the campus' events happen.
